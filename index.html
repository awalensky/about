Who is Brainhack.org?

Board

The board is currently made up of the founders of Brainhack. The board meets quarterly to plan future Brainhack events and discuss other business. Cameron Craddock, director Daniel Margulies Pierre Bellec Nolan Nichols Tal Yarkoni

Current Members

The Brainhack organization is composed of individuals that have previously hosted brainhack events and those that plan to host events in the future. Members include: <> multi column list of all organizers, authors on the brainhack paper, brainhack global organizers
